McLaren P1 Carbon Series

This McLaren P1 Carbon Series render is my first step into a dedicated automotive portfolio, created entirely in Blender. I wanted to capture the car's sleek lines, bold red accents, and the sense of motion even while standing still. Each angle and lighting choice reflects my fascination with motorsport design, precision modeling, and the thrill of engineering high-performance machines.
