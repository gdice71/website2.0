BRANDING & DESIGN

Tasty Treats Bake Stand

I designed this branding piece for Tasty Treats Bake Stand, a Vancouver Island small business specializing in homemade baked goods. The design incorporates a warm, rustic aesthetic to reflect the stand's handmade quality and strong connection to nature.

The logo features rolling mountains, evergreens, and a rising sun, paired with illustrations of bread and pastries to clearly communicate the bake stand's offerings.

*Any other designs/modifications of this logo were not made by me, and I don't support if AI was used to modify the original logo.
