Lando Norris' 1-of-1 MSO
McLaren 765LT Spider

A personal project recreating Lando Norris' 1-of-1 MSO McLaren 765LT Spider, built to explore how light, motion, and design interact in 3D space.

This was my second-ever Blender project and my first time working with animation and cinematic lighting. The goal was to capture the precision, performance, and artistry that define McLaren's design language.

The full study includes both studio lighting sequences and a dynamic wet-asphalt scene rendered at 24 fps.
